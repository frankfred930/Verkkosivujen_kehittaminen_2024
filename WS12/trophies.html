Premier League

Manchester City has won the Premier League nine times, dominating English football in recent years. They are the current Premier League champions.

FA Cup

City has claimed the FA Cup several times, with recent victories adding to their illustrious history in domestic football.

Carabao Cup

The Carabao Cup has been a regular feature of Manchester City's trophy cabinet in recent years, with multiple wins.

UEFA Champions League

The Champions League trophy is the pinnacle of European football, and Manchester City has reached the final two times and won once.

Community Shield

Manchester City has regularly claimed the Community Shield, showcasing their strength as the best in English football.

Other Trophies

Manchester City has also won a number of other trophies, including the UEFA Cup and more, contributing to their rich history.
